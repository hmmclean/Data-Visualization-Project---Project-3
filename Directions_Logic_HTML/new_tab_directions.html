How to Use the Map

Welcome to our map! Follow these instructions to make the most of your experience:

  • Enter your starting address.
  • Click on any park on the map to investigate it further. Zoom in and out with your mouse.
  • Click on the park name in "Travel To" section and it will populate as your final destination.
  • Then click Go!
